# Message Templates

These are starting points — always personalize before sending.

---

## Cold Outreach (LinkedIn / Email)

```
Hi [Name],

[One specific observation about their background — a project, post, company, or skill.]

We're [one sentence on what we're building and why it's relevant to them].

Would you be open to a quick 20-minute chat to learn more? No pressure if the timing isn't right.

[Your name]
```

---

## Application Acknowledgement

```
Hi [Name],

Thanks for applying for the [Role] position at [Company]. We've received your application and will be in touch within [X business days] with next steps.

In the meantime, feel free to [check out our blog / explore our product / etc.].

[Your name]
[Title]
```

---

## Interview Invitation

```
Hi [Name],

Thanks for your interest in [Role]. We'd love to move forward and invite you to a [type of interview] with [interviewer name / team].

Here's what to expect:
- **Format:** [e.g., 45-minute video call]
- **Topics:** [e.g., your background, a short technical discussion]
- **What to prepare:** [e.g., nothing specific / bring questions about the role]

[Scheduling link or proposed times]

Let me know if you have any questions.

[Your name]
```

---

## Rejection After Application (No Interview)

```
Hi [Name],

Thank you for applying for [Role] at [Company]. After reviewing your application, we've decided not to move forward at this time.

[Optional: We were particularly impressed by X, but we're looking for more experience in Y for this specific role.]

We appreciate you taking the time and wish you well in your search.

[Your name]
```

---

## Rejection After Interview

```
Hi [Name],

Thank you for taking the time to interview for [Role]. It was genuinely great to learn more about your background.

After careful consideration, we've decided to move forward with another candidate. [Optional: It was a close decision — we were impressed by X.]

We'd love to stay in touch [if genuine — e.g., "especially as we grow the team in X area"].

Thanks again, and best of luck.

[Your name]
```

---

## Offer Letter Intro

```
Hi [Name],

I'm excited to share that we'd like to extend you an offer for the [Role] position at [Company].

[Attach or share formal offer document.]

I'll be in touch shortly to walk through the details and answer any questions. In the meantime, feel free to reach out anytime.

[Your name]
```
