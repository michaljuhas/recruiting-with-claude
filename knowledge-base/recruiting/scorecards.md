# Interview Scorecards

## How We Score

Use a consistent scale across all roles:


| Score | Label      | Meaning                                     |
| ----- | ---------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| 4     | Strong Yes | Exceptional signal, would advocate for hire |
| 3     | Yes        | Clear positive signal, meets the bar        |
| 2     | No         | Does not meet the bar for this role         |
| 1     | Strong No  | Significant concern, would advocate against |


Avoid "Maybe" / "Leaning Yes" — every interviewer must take a position.

---

## Universal Criteria (All Roles)

These are evaluated by at least one interviewer in every loop.

### Communication

- Explains complex ideas clearly
- Listens and adapts
- Gives and receives feedback well

### Problem-Solving

- Breaks down ambiguous problems
- Shows reasoning, not just answers
- Knows when to ask for help

### Values Alignment

- Consistent with our stated values (see `company/culture.md`)
- No red flags: blaming others, inability to admit mistakes

### Motivation

- Genuine interest in our specific problem space
- Not just fleeing a bad situation
- Has done research on the company

| --------------- | ----------- | ----------------------------------------- |

| Criterion           | Weight | Notes                                 |
| ------------------- | ------ | ------------------------------------- |
