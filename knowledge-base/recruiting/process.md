# Hiring Process

## Overview


| Stage                         | Who            | SLA                               |
| ----------------------------- | -------------- | --------------------------------- |
| Application review            | Recruiter      | 3 business days                   |
| Recruiter screen              | Recruiter      | Within 1 week of application      |
| Hiring manager screen         | HM             | Within 1 week of recruiter screen |
| Technical / Skills assessment | Team           | Within 1 week                     |
| Final interview loop          | Panel          | Within 1 week                     |
| Offer                         | Recruiter + HM | Within 48h of final decision      |

- What we look for: [minimum bar criteria]

### 2. Recruiter Screen (30 min)

**Goals:** Assess motivation, logistics fit, and basic role understanding.

Topics covered:

- Why are they looking / why now?
- What do they know about us?
- Logistics: location, start date, comp expectations
- Quick overview of the role

### 3. Hiring Manager Screen (45–60 min)

**Goals:** Assess cultural fit and role-specific depth.

## Candidate Experience Standards

- No ghosting: respond to every application within [X days]
- Provide feedback when declined after interview
- Keep candidates updated at every stage
