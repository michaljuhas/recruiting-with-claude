| Channel                        | Best For                    | Notes                                           |
| ------------------------------ | --------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| LinkedIn Recruiter             | Most roles                  | Best for passive candidates                     |
| GitHub / GitLab                | Engineers                   | Look at contribution quality, not just activity |
| Dribbble / Behance             | Designers                   | Portfolio is the application                    |
| AngelList / Wellfound          | Startup-oriented candidates | Self-selects for risk tolerance                 |
| Referrals                      | All roles                   | Highest conversion, prioritize                  |
| Job boards (Lever, Greenhouse) | Active candidates           | Don't ignore — often undervalued                |
| Community Slack / Discord      | Niche roles                 | Find communities specific to the skill set      |
| Conferences / Meetups          | Senior roles                | Build relationships before you need to hire     |

## Passive vs. Active Candidates

**Passive candidates** (not actively looking):

- Require more personalization and a compelling pitch
- Need a reason to consider leaving — focus on impact and growth
- Expect a higher-touch process

**Active candidates** (open to new roles):

- Already motivated — don't over-sell
- Move faster — respond quickly or you'll lose them
- Check what they're looking for; they may have other offers
